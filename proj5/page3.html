Creating a Healthy Work-from-Home Experience

  1. Designate a workplace in your home
    1. Set up a spot in your home for work to create a boundary between professional and personal life
  2. Establish a routine
    1. Stick to a consistent schedule to maintain structure and decrease distractions
  3. Set boundaries
    1. Communicate with your friends and family to minimize outside distractions
  4. Prioritize self-care
    1. Make sure to continue to exercise and take up hobbies in your daily routine
